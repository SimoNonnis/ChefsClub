Visiting Chefs 2015

Cara Stadler

Food & Wine Best New Chef 2014, Cara Stadler, hopped all over the globe, from Berkeley and Philadelphia to Versailles, Singapore, Shanghai and Beijing, before landing in Portland, ME, where she opened Tao Yuan together with her mother in 2011. Here, the duo melds flavors from all around Asia with the best New England products that surround them.

Paul Qui

Food & Wine Best New Chef 2014 and Top Chef Season 9 winner Paul Qui helms the stoves at Qui, the street food-focused East Side King, and a soon-to-open sushi bar called Otoko in Austin, TX. Born in the Philippines, trained in Japanese kitchens, and having travelled extensively, Paul blends many influences into a style that is uniquely his own.

Greg & Gabrielle Denton

Food & Wine Best New Chefs 2014, Greg and Gabrielle Denton, are masters of fire. Their restaurant Ox in Portland, Oregon recalls an Argentinian steakhouse, but explores the culinary traditions of Spain, France and Italy, while keeping its roots firmly planted in the Pacific Northwest.
